Investment Sales

Altitude Commercial Real Estate uses research-driven transaction solutions to ensure our investors’ success in selling and buying investment-grade properties and portfolios.

We offer strategic property positioning, performance driven marketing programs, and analysis and advisory to capitalize on each investor’s assets and best meet their needs.

These customized services, along with our investment professionals’ expertise in all major market sectors, are ideal for investors of all types. Whether you’re an individual looking to break into the real estate holdings market, an investor wanting to divest assets or portfolios, or a large corporation with assets in the millions of dollars, Altitude Commercial Real Estate customizes the right strategy and solution to fit your real estate and investment goals.

Investment knowledge

Our team of experts are committed to staying up to the minute on market data at local and global levels. They understand the ins and outs of real estate investment and offer a depth of knowledge to investors looking for reliable, effective guidance in their real estate transactions.

Acquisition

Whether your goals involve short, medium, or long-term gain investments, our experts can determine optimal investments opportunities. Whether residential, commercial, or industrial, we have experts across all market sectors, so we can identify your best options and build a strategy for their acquisition.

Disposition

At times, it can be in an owner’s best interest to release investments that haven’t served them well, or to divest from an asset for the greatest opportunity to realize gains. If this is your situation, our experts can help you maximize your return on these investments while avoiding potential risks.

How we can help

Strategic Property Positioning

Property positioning is what gets buyers in the door. Will the buyers’ attention be captured by the vision of future opportunities, or will they be distracted by minor, unimportant details accidentally emphasized by an agent? We start the process of selling the property by developing its strategic positioning profile.

From the way the property is presented, to its story (both in print, and in photos and promotional video), its highlights, to its financials and marketing materials, everything in our sales package gets built around one target: the most likely buyer for this specific property and the unique ways of catching that buyer’s attention.

Performance Driven Marketing Programs

Performance-driven marketing combines quality exposure to widespread broker and buyer communities with direct marketing to the smaller pools of most likely buyers and accountability to the seller.

We understand the need of the property owner to be kept updated on the progress of the sale. Our bi-weekly marketing report quantifies marketing efforts and their actual results.

Offer Analysis and Advisory

When multiple offers come in, it can be difficult to decide which is best. Sometimes the one with the highest price tag is not the one most likely to close.

Our proprietary Offer Analysis matrix system provides analytical tools to our clients to help them make the best decisions on which offer to accept.

Transaction Management Systems

Time kills deals. But, by following a project-based methodology of transaction management, we can drive a deal to closing with less re-trading and more successful closings. Moreover, it cuts the risk of things getting missed and needing to put your property back on the market to find another buyer.

Customized strategy

Our highest priority is meeting your unique real estate needs. Our team uses the most recent data for capital flows, emerging trends, and the state of the market to develop a successful, customized strategy to help you meet your investment and asset goals.

Experience and Resources

We are a boutique office with a very broad reach. With offices in the over 200 locations across the United States and a strong presence internationally, the collaborative culture of the SVN allows our team to share specific, local expertise. Combined, this expertise leverages global market knowledge and local investment insight to enable the success of our investors across all market sectors.
