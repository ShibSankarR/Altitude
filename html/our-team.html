  • Astute negotiator known for delivering superior outcomes
  • Respected Commercial Real Estate Broker, MBA, and Real Estate Attorney
  • Uses deep market knowledge and cutting-edge tools to find, shape, and close deals
  • Reputation for developing innovative solutions for the most challenging issues backed up with real-world analysis
  • Focused on delivering reliable capital accumulating results for high net-worth individuals, wealth managers and institutional investors

Known for his ability to successfully work with entrepreneurs, property owners, celebrities and international business leaders, Tilden has been able to create stellar deals for his clients in both the good times and the bad. His clients have come to depend on his integrated approach – every transaction is a part of the larger picture that is meant to maximize the value of the property, while increasing one’s net worth. He has handled more than 400 high net worth clients since 2004.

Josh Snyder

NNN Specialist
  • My listings

Josh Snyder specializes in the sales and acquisitions of dialysis clinics and single-tenant investments across the country. His attention to detail, work ethic and integrity allows him to best serve his clients on a national level.

Client relationships are of the utmost importance to Josh, which is why he works on building lasting relationships throughout the years with his customer base. He believes that a happy client is a repeat client and thus is always one step ahead of the competition by putting the clients’ needs on top of the priority list. Josh’s focus on research and market data gives him the competitive edge when it comes to providing information to his clients.

Josh is a graduate of Willamette University in Salem, Oregon where he received a Bachelor’s Degree in English. Before launching his career in commercial real estate, Josh worked in motion picture marketing in the film industry for various studios, including The Walt Disney Studios and Universal Pictures. His time working in entertainment allowed Josh to utilize his intense work ethic where he thrived in a demanding and fast-paced environment. These same attributes have contributed to his continued and growing success in commercial real estate.

Christian Hayes

Retail and Office Specialist
  • My listings

Christian Hayes began his career in 1990 with Windsor Financial Corporation (a developer of convenience shopping centers throughout Southern California) starting in site acquisitions, then later business development, leasing, property management, and dispositions. Mr. Hayes currently specializes in leasing and investment sales, representing both businesses and building owners primarily for retail, medical, and industrial properties for the Fernando and Conejo valleys. He represents numerous retail property owners and tenants in negotiating lease transactions, sales and acquisitions. Mr. Hayes’ expertise brought him recognition from large retailers like Jack in the Box, The Daily Grind, and Subway.

Notably, he represented AH Warner Center Properties LLC, with CB Richard Ellis, in the leasing of a 1.8 million square foot, Class A, high rise project in Woodland Hills, where he completed over 40 lease transactions. He is consistently on the cutting edge of commercial real estate leasing and investment sales strategies and responds to the needs of his clients with acuity and expertise.
